In today’s complex digital landscape, knowing your vulnerabilities before attackers do is critical. At Cloud Guardians, our Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) services are designed to give you a clear picture of your security posture—identifying weaknesses, simulating real-world attacks, and providing actionable insights to strengthen your defenses.

A Vulnerability Assessment scans your systems, applications, and networks to uncover potential security flaws, from misconfigurations to outdated software. But discovery alone isn’t enough. That’s where Penetration Testing comes in—our experts simulate the tactics of real attackers to validate risks, demonstrate impact, and test your ability to respond. Together, VAPT provides both breadth and depth, ensuring no blind spot goes unchecked.

Our VAPT services cover:

  • Network & Infrastructure Testing: Identifying entry points in internal and external networks.
  • Web & Mobile Application Testing: Finding weaknesses in business-critical applications.
  • Cloud Environment Testing: Securing workloads and configurations across AWS, Azure, and GCP.
  • Wireless & IoT Security: Assessing devices and access points often overlooked.
  • Remediation Guidance: Clear, prioritized recommendations to close gaps quickly and effectively.
